About



Hello! I am a 5th year Computer Science PhD student at Stanford University.
I am part of the Stanford Natural Language Processing Group and advised by Dan Jurafsky.
Before graduate school I recieved a BS from Williams College.

My goal is to build socially aware systems that support human interaction at scale. My research has touched on text-based causal inference, machine translation, bias, fairness, and neural network interpretability.
