About



I am a research scientist at Microsoft.
I completed a PhD in Computer Science at Stanford University, advised by Dan Jurafsky.
I recieved my BS from Williams College.

I'm interested in building socially aware NLP systems as motivated by
applications in machine translation, causal inference, algorithmic fairness and interpretability.

Last updated 6/11/21 -- please see my Google Scholar for a complete list of publications.
